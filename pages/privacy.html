1. Data Collection

DOMPrompter does not collect personal identity data by default. All core functionalities — element selection, style adjustment, and prompt generation — operate locally on your device, not on remote servers.

2. Local Storage

DOMPrompter may store project files and operation history locally to support undo/redo and style diff tracking. These files can be deleted at any time within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

DOMPrompter communicates with browsers via the Chrome DevTools Protocol. It does not send page data to any third-party services. Generated AI prompts are manually copy-pasted by you into AI tools — DOMPrompter itself does not connect to any AI service directly.
